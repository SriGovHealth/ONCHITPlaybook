ONC Patient Engagement Playbook

Patient portals can have big benefits for your practice and your patients. They’re tools for patient involvement that can lead to better communication, better care, and better outcomes.

Make your portal work for you — and use the Patient Engagement Playbook to help you get there. The Playbook is an evolving resource: a compilation of tips, hacks, and workarounds we learned from innovative providers and health systems like yours.
